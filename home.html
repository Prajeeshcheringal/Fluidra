About us

FOUNDED IN 1969

Fluidra Group is a listed Spanish multinational group devoted to developing application for the sustainable use of water at the service of people´s wellbeing and health, leisure, sports and therapeutic applications associated with the extensive field of Pool & Wellness for domestic, commercial and public use.

FOUNDED IN 1995

Fluidra Middle East is one of 46+ branch offices for Fluidra GROUP (The Spanish multinational group with two headquarters in Barcelona & San Diego). We are located in Jafza (Jebel Ali Free Zone), Dubai, UAE. In addition to offices, a big warehouse with more than 6,500 sq. mtrs. We have a skilled team with 40+ members in Management, Business Development, Sales, Financial, and Logistics Departments; Managed by Jose Nascimento (Managing Director of Fluidra Middle East & India).

Our sales territory area covers all Gulf Countries (UAE, KSA, OMAN, QATAR, KUWAIT, and BAHRAIN) in addition to other middle east countries (JORDAN, SRI LANKA, MALDIVES and YEMEN).

Olympics Pools

More than 45 years of experience in designing and equipping competition and training pools is the best possible endorsement of compliance with international standards.

Fluidra is a big player at major sports events. It has taken part in the design, planning and execution of many facilities used for World Aquatics Championships run by FINA, such as Barcelona 2003, Shanghai 2006, Melbourne 2007 and Manchester 2008, in addition to the 2018 Mediterranean Games.

Fluidra's Industry Division supplies equipment to Fluidra Middle East and centralizes the management of production in Europe. Fluidra dominates nine production technologies: plastic injection, plastic extrusion, fibreglass spraying, stainless steel handling, glass coating, thermoplastic blow-moulding, rubber transformation, solid chlorine production and process engineering.

The company is specialized in solutions for swimming pool, wellness, irrigation, water treatment and applied fluid handling.
